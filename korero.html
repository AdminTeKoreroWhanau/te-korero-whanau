Kōrero

Gather video interviews, audio kōrero, and photo montages. Share authentic narratives: history, ambitions, and ties to whenua.

How can I contribute a kōrero?

Record audio/video on your phone and share via cloud link, or write a short story. Include names, places, and dates if known.

Tāpiri kōrero / Add a story or vlog

By default, posts are stored on your device. Configure Supabase in assets/backend.js to share with whānau.

Ngā tāurunga / Posts

Kāore anō he tāurunga. Tāpiri tētahi kōrero, vlog rānei ki runga ake nei.
